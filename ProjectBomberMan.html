Bomber-Man

Throughout The semester we had to build this engine (Overlord Engine) back up, as it was missing a lot of functionallity. This consisetd of implementing multiple features, these are some of them:
Sprite Rendering, Text Rendering, Picking, Software animation, Hardware animation, Shadow mapping, Particle System, Multiple Post-Processing effects, Lighting, Parsing sprite font.
Afterwards we were asked to make a game with the engine we had put together to prove that our implementations worked. With the time-frame we were given, I settled on making a Bomber-Man clone, as the scope of it didn't seem to large '<-- Rephrase this'.
Shaders were implemented using DirectX11 with the Effects system.
