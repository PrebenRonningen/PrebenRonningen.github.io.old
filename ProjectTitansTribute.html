  • Meltem Ozcelik - Art, Character Art & UI
  • Preben Rønningen(Me) - Programming, UI Implementation, Gameplay
  • Zenn Geeraerts - Programming, Gameplay
  • Thomas Vincent - Programmin, Generalist

After production had begun I was primarily involved in implementing UI. However when the UI was in a working state my focus switched to gameplay.
